Strategic Product Placement Analysis

A data analytics project using Tableau to evaluate how product placement influences supermarket sales performance.

Project Overview:

This project focuses on analyzing how strategic product placement influences supermarket sales performance. By examining structured retail sales data, the study aims to uncover patterns that impact customer purchasing behavior and revenue generation.

The analysis involves systematic data preprocessing including cleaning, transformation, and organization of sales datasets to ensure accuracy and reliability of results.

Using Tableau dashboards and interactive stories, the project visualizes sales trends, category performance, seasonal demand variations, and promotional effects. These insights support informed business decisions and optimized product positioning strategies.

The final analytical solution is integrated into a Flask-based web interface for structured presentation and professional deployment.
